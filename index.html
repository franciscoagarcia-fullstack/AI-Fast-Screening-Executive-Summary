FullStack · Talent Operations · Methodology

Agentic Talent Fast Screening

A two-layer system: an AI agent reasons over every applicant against fixed guidelines, then a deterministic engine converts its findings into a single, auditable 1–5 score — consistent at scale, in seconds.
Stage 1 · Gather
📥 The Evidence
Authoritative inputs assembled per candidate & role.
📄
Resume
History, tenure, education, certifications
🧩
Skills
Declared skills vs. the role's required skills
💬
Application Q&A
Work auth, location, experience, role fit
Reasons only from real data — never fabricates.
→
Stage 2 · Reason
🧠 AI Evaluation
The agent scores four weighted dimensions, 0–100 each.
Skill Matchweight 50%
Experience Relevanceweight 20%
Q&A Alignmentweight 20%
Education & Certsweight 10%
🚧 Required-skill gate: any must-have skill missing → candidate is capped at Score 1, regardless of the rest.
→
Stage 3 · Compute
⚙️ Scoring Engine
Component scores combine into a weighted strength score.
Strength = 0.50·Skill + 0.20·Exp + 0.20·Q&A + 0.10·Edu
Same math, every candidate — fully reproducible
Skill match < 80% · missing required→ 1
Strength < 70→ 2
Strength < 80→ 3
Strength < 90→ 4
Strength ≥ 90→ 5
AI reasons · the engine decides — no black-box scoring.
→
